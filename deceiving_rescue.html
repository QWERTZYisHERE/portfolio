Deceiving Rescue

Our team developed an innovative game within an oscillator! This endless scrolling adventure, titled Deceiving Rescue, follows a brave king on a mission to save a princess from a treacherous cave—only to discover she didn’t actually need rescuing. To bring this clever concept to life, we leveraged JavaScript libraries such as P5.js and XYScope. Although the models for the boss, king, and princess were still in development, they integrated seamlessly with the oscillator, showcasing the potential of this unique approach.
