Mitte Basel

An interactive data visualization project that explores the development of the city of Basel, Switzerland, from 2012 to 2025. The project combines population data and urban data to create an engaging and informative experience for users interested in the evolution of Basel's population changes.

  • Team: Amelie Schroeder
  • Tool: Touchdesigner 2026
  • Role: 3D Art and Backend Development

Marbelous Maze

Our main idea is to make a board game digitally interactive with the help of the AprilTag Library. The board game is based on Marble Maze, in which two players must collaborate to prevent the marbles from falling out of the maze. The maze is also customizable by rearranging its sections. In the digital version, the marbles interact with an alphabet grid. If interested, the whole documentation of the project can be found here.

  • Mentoring: Studio Unstated
  • Team: Anna Rieder
  • Tool: AprilTag Library, JavaScript
  • Role: Product Design and Debugging
  • Year: 2025

Project Westfeld

Together, we brought the Westfeld Schiff building to life. We aimed to showcase the two sides of Westfeld Schiff: the old exterior architecture and the newly renovated interior. In our film, we animated the building's most recognizable characteristics.

  • Team: Taylor Kovacevic
  • Tool: Cinema 4D

Real/Fake

This project explores the relevance of image and video manipulation on social media, specifically focusing on beauty filters in apps. Through a series of posters, the project aims to raise awareness of how seamlessly such manipulations can alter perceptions in our digital world.

  • Mentoring: Dirk Koy
  • Actors: Senem Arkaz, Mikail Arkaz, Turan Efe Arkaz
  • Tool: Adobe After Effects
  • Role: Camera Work, Animation
  • Year: 2025
