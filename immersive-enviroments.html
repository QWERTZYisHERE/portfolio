Immersive Environment

For this project, I created an interactive 3D environment using the Unity game engine. The environment is inspired by the passage of the Oslo building, capturing its unique architectural details. All 3D models were crafted in Cinema 4D, ensuring an accurate and visually engaging representation. If you'd like to experience it for yourself, feel free to give it atry!.
